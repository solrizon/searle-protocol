On-Chain Attribution Addendum (-OCA) — Searle Protocol License v1.0

This addendum applies when the -OCA suffix is appended to any Searle
Protocol License variant (e.g., Searle-NT-OCA-1.0).

1. ON-CHAIN REGISTRATION. The Creator has registered this Work on a public
   blockchain via the Searle Protocol smart contracts or a compatible
   attribution registry. The on-chain record includes:
   - Content hash of the Work
   - Creator's blockchain address
   - Registration timestamp
   - License variant identifier

2. EVIDENTIARY VALUE. The on-chain record serves as supplementary evidence
   of authorship and licensing terms. In case of disputes, the earliest
   on-chain record with a matching content hash is considered prima facie
   evidence of prior creation. See Article 4.3 of the full license text.

3. ATTRIBUTION REFERENCE. Distributed copies of the Work SHOULD include
   or reference the on-chain attribution identifier in the format:
   [CHAIN:CONTRACT_ADDRESS:ASSET_ID]

4. INTEGRITY. Tampering with, removing, or falsifying on-chain attribution
   records constitutes a material breach of the underlying license.

This addendum does not modify any other terms of the base license variant.
