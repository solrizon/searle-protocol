This addendum applies when the -WM suffix is appended to any Searle
Protocol License variant (e.g., Searle-NT-WM-1.0).

1. WATERMARK NOTICE. Distributed copies of this Work contain visible,
   invisible, steganographic, or metadata-based watermarks that uniquely
   identify the Recipient, distribution timestamp, or distribution channel.

2. PROHIBITED ACTIONS. The Recipient SHALL NOT:
   (a) Remove, alter, obscure, or attempt to reverse-engineer any watermark;
   (b) Distribute de-watermarked copies of the Work;
   (c) Use technical means to circumvent watermark detection.

3. EVIDENTIARY VALUE. Watermark detection constitutes admissible evidence
   that a specific copy of the Work was distributed to a specific Recipient.
   See Article 5 of the full license text.

4. CONSEQUENCES OF VIOLATION. Unauthorized removal or circumvention of
   watermarks may trigger:
   (a) License termination under Article 8;
   (b) On-chain penalty proceedings, if applicable;
   (c) Civil liability under applicable copyright law.

5. NO NEGATIVE INFERENCE. The absence of a detectable watermark does not
   imply the absence of restrictions. All license terms apply regardless.
